Inside the technology

Our gloves integrate three AI models that work together to predict and prevent accidents.

Lane detection

Vision‑based model identifies lane markings and vehicle position in real time, even in low light.

Depth estimation

Monocular depth estimation calculates distance to obstacles with high accuracy using a single camera.

Collision probability

Assigns a risk score to every vehicle based on trajectory and speed – alerts only when necessary.
